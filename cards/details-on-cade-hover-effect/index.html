Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and the older brother of Nezuko Kamado. He has tanned skin, an athletic physique, and ruffled, black hair with burgundy tips.

Senku Ishigami

Senku Ishigami is the protagonist from Riichiro Inagaki and Boichi's manga series Dr. Stone. Beginning in April 5738 AD, it has been over 3,700 years since a mysterious flash petrified nearly all human life.

Ben Tennyson

Benjamin Kirby "Ben" Tennyson, commonly known as Ben 10, is a fictional superhero and the title protagonist of the Ben 10 franchise.
